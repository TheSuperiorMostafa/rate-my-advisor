# DMCA Takedown Policy Outline

## Structure for DMCA Policy Page

### 1. Overview

Rate My Advisor respects intellectual property rights and complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA).

### 2. Reporting Copyright Infringement

To file a DMCA takedown notice, provide:

1. **Identification of Copyrighted Work**
   - Description of copyrighted material
   - Location of original work (URL, etc.)

2. **Identification of Infringing Material**
   - Specific URL(s) of infringing content
   - Description of infringing content

3. **Contact Information**
   - Your name, address, phone, email
   - Statement of good faith belief
   - Statement of accuracy under penalty of perjury

4. **Signature**
   - Physical or electronic signature

### 3. Where to Send Notice

**DMCA Agent:**
- Email: dmca@ratemyadvisor.com
- Mail: [Your Address]
- Response time: 48 hours

### 4. Counter-Notification

If content was removed, you may file a counter-notification:

1. Identification of removed content
2. Statement of good faith belief
3. Consent to jurisdiction
4. Contact information
5. Signature

### 5. Repeat Infringers

- Accounts with multiple violations may be terminated
- Policy: 3 strikes = account termination

### 6. Process

1. **Receipt** - Notice received and logged
2. **Review** - Verified for completeness
3. **Action** - Content removed if valid
4. **Notification** - User notified of removal
5. **Counter-Notice** - User may file counter-notice
6. **Restoration** - Content restored if counter-notice valid (after 10-14 days)

### 7. False Claims

- False DMCA claims may result in liability
- Knowingly false statements are perjury

### 8. Contact

**DMCA Agent:**
- Email: dmca@ratemyadvisor.com
- Subject: "DMCA Takedown Request"
