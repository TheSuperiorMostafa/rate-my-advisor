## Versioning Strategy

### Semantic Versioning

- **Major (X.0.0)** - Breaking changes, major features
- **Minor (0.X.0)** - New features, backward compatible
- **Patch (0.0.X)** - Bug fixes, minor improvements

### Release Schedule

- **MVP (v1.0.0)** - Launch version
- **v1.1.0** - 3 months post-launch
- **v2.0.0** - 6-12 months post-launch
- **v2.1.0** - 12-18 months post-launch

### Feature Prioritization

1. **High Priority** - User-requested, high impact
2. **Medium Priority** - Nice-to-have, moderate impact
3. **Low Priority** - Future consideration
