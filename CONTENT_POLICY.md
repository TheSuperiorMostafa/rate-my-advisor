## Full Version (For Content Policy Page)

### Our Commitment

Rate My Advisor provides a platform for students to share honest, constructive feedback about academic advisors. We maintain strict content policies to ensure a safe and respectful environment.

### What's Allowed

✅ **Professional Feedback**
- Honest assessments of advisor performance
- Specific examples of interactions
- Constructive criticism
- Recommendations based on experience

✅ **Ratings & Categories**
- Accuracy, Responsiveness, Helpfulness
- Availability, Advocacy, Clarity
- Meeting type and timeframe information

### What's Not Allowed

❌ **Personal Information**
- Email addresses, phone numbers, addresses
- Any identifying contact information

❌ **Threats & Violence**
- Threats of harm or violence
- Explicit threats against anyone

❌ **Medical Information**
- Medical diagnoses or health details
- Information about medical conditions

❌ **Criminal Accusations**
- Accusations of illegal activity
- Unsubstantiated criminal claims

❌ **Hate Speech**
- Profanity, slurs, discriminatory language
- Content targeting protected characteristics

❌ **Off-Topic Content**
- Reviews not about advising services
- Personal attacks unrelated to performance
- Spam or promotional content

### Enforcement

- All reviews are moderated before publication
- Violations result in review rejection
- Repeated violations may result in account restrictions
- Severe violations may result in legal action

### Reporting

Report inappropriate content using the "Report" button on any review. Reports are reviewed within 24 hours.
