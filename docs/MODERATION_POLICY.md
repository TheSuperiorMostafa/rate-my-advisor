# Moderation Policy

## Overview

Rate My Advisor is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and constructive environment for students to share their experiences with academic advisors. All reviews are moderated to ensure compliance with our content guidelines.

## Review Guidelines

### ✅ Allowed Content

- Honest feedback about advisor performance
- Specific examples of positive or negative interactions
- Constructive criticism about advising services
- Recommendations or warnings based on experience
- Ratings for: Accuracy, Responsiveness, Helpfulness, Availability, Advocacy, Clarity

### ❌ Prohibited Content

1. **Personal Information**
   - Email addresses, phone numbers, physical addresses
   - Any contact information that could identify individuals

2. **Threats & Violence**
   - Threats of harm or violence
   - Explicit threats against advisors or others

3. **Medical Information**
   - Medical diagnoses or health information
   - Details about medical conditions

4. **Criminal Accusations**
   - Accusations of illegal activity
   - Unsubstantiated claims of crimes

5. **Hate Speech & Discrimination**
   - Profanity, slurs, or discriminatory language
   - Content targeting protected characteristics

6. **Off-Topic Content**
   - Reviews not about advising services
   - Personal attacks unrelated to professional performance
   - Spam or promotional content

7. **False Information**
   - Knowingly false statements
   - Fabricated experiences

## Moderation Process

1. **Automatic Filtering**
   - Reviews are automatically scanned for prohibited content
   - Flagged reviews require manual review

2. **Manual Review**
   - All reviews are reviewed by moderators
   - Reviews are approved, rejected, or flagged for further review

3. **Appeal Process**
   - Users can contact support to appeal moderation decisions
   - Appeals reviewed within 48 hours

## Review Status

- **Pending** - Awaiting moderation review
- **Approved** - Published and visible on advisor profile
- **Rejected** - Violates guidelines, not published
- **Flagged** - Requires additional review

## Enforcement

- First violation: Review rejected
- Repeated violations: User may be blocked from submitting reviews
- Severe violations: Immediate blocking and potential legal action

## Reporting Reviews

Users can report reviews that violate guidelines. Reports are reviewed within 24 hours.

## Contact

For moderation questions or appeals:
- Email: moderation@ratemyadvisor.com
- Response time: 24-48 hours
