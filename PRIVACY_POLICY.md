# Privacy Policy Outline

## Structure for Privacy Policy Page

### 1. Introduction
- What information we collect
- How we use information
- Your rights

### 2. Information We Collect

**Personal Information:**
- Email addresses (for authentication)
- .edu email addresses (for verification, hashed)
- IP addresses (for rate limiting and security)

**User-Generated Content:**
- Reviews and ratings
- Reports submitted
- Moderation actions (admin only)

**Automatically Collected:**
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Usage analytics (via Vercel Analytics)

### 3. How We Use Information

- Provide and improve services
- Authenticate users
- Moderate content
- Prevent abuse and spam
- Comply with legal obligations

### 4. Information Sharing

- **Not shared** with third parties except:
  - Service providers (Vercel, Supabase/Neon)
  - Legal requirements (court orders, etc.)
  - With your consent

### 5. Data Security

- Encryption in transit (HTTPS)
- Hashed passwords
- Hashed .edu emails
- Secure database connections

### 6. Data Retention

- Reviews: Retained indefinitely (unless deleted)
- User accounts: Retained while account active
- Logs: Retained for 90 days
- Backups: Retained per backup policy

### 7. Your Rights

- Access your data
- Delete your account
- Request data export
- Opt out of analytics

### 8. Children's Privacy

- Service not intended for users under 13
- We do not knowingly collect data from children

### 9. Changes to Policy

- Policy may be updated
- Users notified of material changes
- Continued use constitutes acceptance

### 10. Contact

- Privacy questions: privacy@ratemyadvisor.com
- Data requests: data@ratemyadvisor.com
